Direwolves: Mythical Guardians

Did you know direwolves were real prehistoric animals? In stories like Game of Thrones, they’re brave companions. Scientists found fossils showing they were larger than modern wolves!

Playing sports helps kids stay active, build friendships, and have fun! Football, basketball or even tag counts — just move and smile!

Pixel Dash is a new 2D jumping game where you collect stars and avoid lava! It’s colorful, fast, and super fun. Try beating your friends’ high scores.

Tech Bit: What is a Smartwatch?

Smartwatches can track steps, call your friends, and even tell jokes. They are mini-computers for your wrist!
